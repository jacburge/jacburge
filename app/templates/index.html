My name is Jaclyn. Nice to meet you!
A California native, I currently live in Seattle working as a data analyst after graduating from UC Berkeley with a degree in Cognitive Science. As a "philomath," I am always in the pursuit of mastering new skills, sharing and acquiring knowledge, and freelancing for fun side projects. I enjoy spending my time:
  • finding meaning from numbers

  • working on web development and design projects

  • studying Internal Chinese Martial Arts (tai chi + baguazhang )

  • playing piano and sharing music with communities (including senior homes, weddings, and church services; personal favorites: Chopin, Debussy, Beethoven, Eiunadi; may also be found jamming with my djembe or other multicultural percussion instruments)

  • being a classic introvert (painting, sketching, reading, journaling, coffee shop hopping, meditating, and traveling solo)

  • exchanging handwritten pen pal letters

  • teaching, tutoring, and mentoring

  • contemplating and discussing ideas surrounding topics such as quantum consciousness, education accessibility, minimalist living, personality types, and improving quality of life for elders

  • and making lists. For everything.
